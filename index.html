Experience life of our Panda Joey!

Our Panda Joey is a jolly good fellow who is on a journey to collect as much food till next summer and gifts until next Christmas!
But yes, no journey was ever made easy! Help our little friend Joey collect all his goodies by overcoming all the obstacles on his way. Joey wishes you Good Luck !

Gameplay Features

Realistic Physics

Look out for the cars coming from all directions, you might not want Joey to die ! Beware of stepping into water as well.

Exciting Advantage

Get an exciting advantage on collection of rewards. Make use of this advantage to help Joey fulfil his motive of collecting food and gifts for himself !

Easy Reset

Want to replay the same level or switch to another level? Don't worry, we have made it really easy to navigate !

Cool Sound Effects

The Panda Company made sure to give the gamers a real fun experience by adding interesting sound effects at various points in the game. We made sure not to bore you at any point !
